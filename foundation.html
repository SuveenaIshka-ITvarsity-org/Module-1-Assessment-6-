MAC FOUNDATION

DESCRIPTION

The Face And Body Foundation by MAC is lightweight, moisturizing, and long-wearing. It delivers sheer coverage with a satin finish. The foundation glides onto your face and body, providing true-to-color even skin tone. The MAC Face And Body Foundation comes in 13 different shades.The 'N' stands for neutral, which tones down the amount of warmth or coolness in the foundation. That means an 'NC' shade is less pink than a 'C' shade is. Second, the numbers refer to the lightness or darkness of your skin. The higher the number, the darker the shade
